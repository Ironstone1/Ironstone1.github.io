A map where you can try out different knifes in CSGO and take screenshots.

You can test out all the knifes from butterfly, to karambit, bayonet to falcon, this map has them all! Map also has changeable sky backdrop for taking screenshots and knifes can also be tested on bots too.

How to use:
- Join CT team

Important!
If you have a knife skin for CSGO equipped, you will need to unequip it and equip the stock knife before using this map. Else the knifes in this map will not work.
