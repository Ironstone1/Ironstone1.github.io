A map where you can try out diffrent knifes in CSGO, and take screenshots.

You can test out all the knifes from butterfly, to karambit, bayonet to falcon, this map has them all! Map has changable sky backdrop for taking screenshots. Knifes can also be tested on bots.

How to use:
- Join CT team

Important!
If you have a knife skin for CSGO equiped, you will need to unequip it and equip the stock knife before using this map. Else the knifes in this map will not work.
