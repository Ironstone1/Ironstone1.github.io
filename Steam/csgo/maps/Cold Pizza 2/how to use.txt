this map can be used to earn all of the following achievements:
- Keep on Gunning
- Kill of the Century
- The Professional
- King of the Kill
- Cold Pizza Eater

How to use:
1. load the map, through the console by typing "map cold_pizza_achiv demolition".
2. Place a heavy object on your keyboard space bar.

you can also get the Piece Treaty "Win 250 Pistol Rounds in Competitive Mode" achievement, if you load the map in competitive mode.
