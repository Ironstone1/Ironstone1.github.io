This map will help you earn the "Level Playing Field" "Kill an enemy who is on gold knife level with a sub-machine gun in Arms Race Mode" achievement.

This map can also be used to get the "Rampage!" "Win an Arms Race match without dying" and "Conservationist" "Win an Arms Race match without reloading any of your weapons" achievements.

How to use
- Join the Counter Terrorist (CT) team

Towards the end, once the terrorist bot gets the golden knife (or last pistol), both bots will teleport to a diffrent part of the map, at this point just wait, the bots will teleport back in 20 seconds.
